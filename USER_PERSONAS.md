# User Personas

## Persona 1: Alex - The Busy Professional (Casual Viewer)
- **Age:** 32
- **Occupation:** Software Engineer
- **Goal:** Wants to quickly find a highly-rated movie to watch on Friday night without spending 30 minutes browsing.
- **Frustrations:** Slow-loading websites, intrusive ads, confusing navigation.
- **Tech Savvy:** High. Expects modern SaaS-level responsiveness.

## Persona 2: Sarah - The Film Enthusiast (Cinephile & Organizer)
- **Age:** 26
- **Occupation:** Graphic Designer
- **Goal:** Needs to look up the cast of an obscure 90s film and add upcoming indie releases to her watchlist.
- **Frustrations:** Illegible text (low contrast), poorly structured data, losing her watchlist when clearing browser cache.
- **Tech Savvy:** Medium. Appreciates clean, accessible, and structured interfaces.
