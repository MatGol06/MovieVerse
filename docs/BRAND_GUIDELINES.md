# Brand Guidelines

## Tone of Voice
The MovieVerse brand voice is:
1. **Professional:** Clear, concise, and respectful of the user's time.
2. **Direct:** No unnecessary jargon or overly enthusiastic marketing speak.
3. **Helpful:** Error messages should explain exactly what went wrong and how to fix it.

- **Do:** "Could not load movies. Please check your connection."
- **Don't:** "Oopsie! Looks like the movie gremlins ate our cables! 🤪"

## Naming & Terminology
- Use "Watchlist" (not "My Favorites" or "Saved Stuffs").
- Use "Search" (not "Discover" for the input field).
- Use "Details" (not "More Info").

## Logo
- Text-based logomark. Clean, sans-serif typography. High contrast. No 3D effects or gradients.
