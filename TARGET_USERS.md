# Target Users

## Overview
MovieVerse is designed for individuals who actively consume film and television content and require a reliable tool for discovery and tracking.

## User Segments

### 1. The Casual Viewer
- **Behavior:** Watches movies occasionally, usually on weekends.
- **Needs:** Quick recommendations ("What's trending?"). Fast, frictionless UI.
- **Pain Point:** Overwhelmed by too many choices and complex navigation on standard entertainment sites.

### 2. The Cinephile
- **Behavior:** Actively researches movies, directors, and cast members.
- **Needs:** Deep data (exact release dates, full cast lists, precise ratings).
- **Pain Point:** Slow search functionalities and cluttered layouts that hide essential data.

### 3. The Organizer
- **Behavior:** Keeps strict track of what they have watched and what they plan to watch.
- **Needs:** A robust, reliable "Watchlist" feature.
- **Pain Point:** Losing track of movie recommendations across different apps.
